Using the Map

  • Set start & end: Search or click to select points; we’ll draw a proposed path.
  • Crime layers: Toggle heatmap or markers to visualize nearby incidents.
  • Filter: Limit by crime type to focus on specific categories (e.g., Robbery, Assault).
  • Save routes: In the analyzer, name & save a route to reuse later.

Understanding Safety Labels

We count incidents near your path and compute a risk label: Safe, Moderate Risk, Risky. These are advisory and depend on available data.

FAQ

Why does the map show incidents I didn’t hear about?

Public datasets can lag, be reclassified, or be incomplete. We display what’s available at ingest time.

Is the “safer” route always the best?

No—our routes are advisory. Always use your judgment and follow local guidance and laws.

Do you collect personal data?

We avoid collecting personal data for this app. See our Disclaimer for details.
