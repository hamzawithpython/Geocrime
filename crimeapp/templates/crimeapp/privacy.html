Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information GeoCrime collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Using GeoCrime means you agree to this policy and our terms.

Information We Collect

Contact Info

If you use the contact form, we collect your email and message so we can reply.

Location

When you use map/routing, we may use your approximate location to center maps and compute routes. We don’t store precise location by default.

Usage & Device Data

IP address, browser type, and basic logs for security, troubleshooting, and improving the app.

Cookies / Local Storage

Used to keep preferences and improve UX. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve map, routing, and risk visualizations.
  • Respond to messages you send via the contact form.
  • Protect our services from abuse and troubleshoot issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required.

Sharing

We don’t sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers who help us run GeoCrime (e.g., email delivery, hosting).

Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it, unless required by law to keep it longer.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You can disable cookies in your browser.
  • You can contact us to access, update, or delete information you’ve provided.
  • Regional rights (e.g., GDPR/CCPA) may apply; contact us to exercise them.

Children’s Privacy

GeoCrime is not intended for children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect personal data from children.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us.
